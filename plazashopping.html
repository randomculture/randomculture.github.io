Discover Plaza Shopping Niterói: The Largest Shopping Center in the Region!

If you're in Niterói and looking for a place that combines shopping, entertainment, and gastronomy, Plaza Shopping is your best option! Inaugurated in October 1986, this shopping center stands out as the first and most complete in the city, offering a unique experience for all visitors.

A Brief History of Plaza Shopping Niterói

Since its opening, Plaza Shopping has become a landmark in the city, transforming the way Niterói residents shop and have fun. With over 44,000 square meters of Gross Leasable Area (GLA), the shopping center underwent a significant expansion in 2013, adding 14,000 m² to the space, allowing for an even more diverse range of stores and services.

Why Visit Plaza Shopping Niterói?

  • Variety of Stores: With over 300 shops, you'll find renowned brands like M.A.C, Sephora, and Osklen, along with options for every taste and style.
  • Diverse Gastronomy: The Gourmet Space is a true invitation to the palate, featuring exclusive restaurants like Abbraccio, Coco Bambu, and Outback, all with stunning views of Guanabara Bay.
  • Quality Entertainment: Enjoy the city's first cinema complex, operated by Cinemark, featuring 3D technology and XD theaters, ensuring an immersive cinematic experience.
  • Comfort and Convenience: Plaza Shopping offers a wide range of services, including lottery, ATMs, and a Family Space, perfect for parents and children.
