Costão de Itacoatiara

Join me on an exhilarating journey to the stunning Costão de Itacoatiara in Niterói. This impressive hiking destination is known for its breathtaking landscapes and serene ambiance. Explore the wonders of this trail and appreciate the captivating natural beauty along the way!

Campo de São Bento: Niterói's Cultural Gem

Campo de São Bento, established in the 19th century, began as a space for leisure and community gatherings. Originally an agricultural area, it has transformed into a vibrant urban park, reflecting the evolution of Niterói and its relationship with nature.

Exploring the Antarctic Desert

The Antarctic Desert is the largest desert in the world, known for its extreme conditions and unique wildlife. Discover the beauty and challenges of this icy landscape!

Discovering Paquetá Island

Paquetá Island is a hidden gem in Guanabara Bay, known for its charming streets and beautiful beaches. Join me as we explore this tranquil paradise!
